Welcome to Solent School!

Located in the heart of Southampton, Solent School is a beacon of educational excellence since its establishment in 1978. With over four decades of dedication to nurturing young minds, we pride ourselves on our commitment to innovation and fostering student potential.

  • Consistent academic excellence with high student performance in national examinations
  • Recognition for innovative teaching methods and curriculum development
  • Numerous awards and accolades in sports, arts, and extracurricular activities
  • Strong community engagement and partnerships, fostering social responsibility and citizenship among students
  • Graduates' successful transitions to esteemed universities and careers, testament to the school's commitment to student success
